A linguist turned programmer. I received a Bachelor's of Arts from University of California, Davis in Linguistics. Having studied various languages (Spanish, American Sign Language, Japanese, German, French, and Mandarin), I loved languages. A desire to understand language at a fundamental level persuaded me to study semantics and syntax. These two concepts can be applied directly and easily to coding. Thus, here I am. :)
